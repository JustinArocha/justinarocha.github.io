About Me

My name is Justin Arocha, I am a web designer who specializes in content creation and search engine optimization.

Web design was not always my first choice but I had always had a desire and love of making things ever since I was little and coming to college allowed me to be exposed to different subjects including the process of web design which I absolutely loved. I think that after working hard on a big project seeing the fruits of your labor in the final product is feeling that could not be replicated and look for the feeling with every project I work on. The biggest example of that proud feeling was the app we worked on during our senior design class that we were able to present in an open showcase for our school and anyone else interested in taking a look at our projects. The ability to show off and talk so passionately about the app we made is such a memorable experience and hearing the feedback and praise for the work we were able to put together as a first time attempt at something so large is something I look forward taking to wherever I may find myself next.
